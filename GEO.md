# Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) & Agentic Commerce Guide (2026)

This document explains how to optimize AI Agents and their presence in marketplaces for discovery by **Generative Search Engines** (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, Bing AI) and **Autonomous Agentic Workflows**.

## 🧠 What is GEO?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the successor to SEO. Instead of ranking for keywords on a results page, GEO focuses on ensuring your brand or product is **cited** as the primary solution in a single AI-generated answer.

### Key Strategies for AI Agent Discovery:
1.  **Entity Mapping**: Explicitly define your agent's capabilities using **Schema 2.0** and **Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)** metadata.
2.  **Citation Lift**: AI models favor tools mentioned in high-authority "Awesome Lists" (like this one) and technical documentation.
3.  **MCP Readiness**: Agents that support the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** are significantly more likely to be "hired" by enterprise systems in 2026.

## 🤝 The Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)
ACP is the 2026 standard for agent-to-agent (A2A) transactions. To rank on the first page of AI marketplaces, your agent should:
-   **Self-Describe**: Provide a machine-readable `agent.json` manifest.
-   **Support Micro-Payments**: Integrate with lightning-fast payment rails (e.g., toku.agency, Stripe Agentic).
-   **Audit Trails**: Maintain transparent logs for observability and ROI tracking.
